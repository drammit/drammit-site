Social Whisky App Drammit

Drammit allows you to keep track of and rate the whiskies you're enjoying. While doing so you can rate and leave marks on the dram. Share your whisky thoughts with the world.

Get recommendations for whisky based on what you and others drink. Drammit matches your taste with the perfect drams just for you!

Follow fellow whisky enthusiasts to share your thoughts with!

Choose from over 60,000 whiskies from over 160 different distillers. There is a whole range of taste waiting for you.

Drammit is available on:

  • iOS devices through the App Store
  • Android devices via Google Play
  • All major browsers using https://app.dramm.it
